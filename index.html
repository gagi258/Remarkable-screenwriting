Fountain cheat sheet

  

    

      
Scene heading
Start a line with INT. / EXT. (Danish too). Or force any line with a leading dot: .A STRANGE PLACE.

      
Action
Just write normally. Any paragraph that isn't another element is action.

      
Character
A NAME in caps on its own line, blank line above. Force with @: @McKenzie.

      
Dialogue
Lines directly under a character.

      
Parenthetical
A line in (parentheses) under the character.

      
Dual dialogue
Add ^ after the second character's name.

      
Transition
An ALL-CAPS line ending in TO:, or force with >. Centered: >THE END<.

      
Emphasis
*italics*, **bold**, _underline_.

      
Sequence cards
Become # Section headers in the script.

      
Danish & bilingual
æ/ø/å supported. i./e. → INT./EXT., d./n. → day/night word. DA/EN toggle in the writing panel.
